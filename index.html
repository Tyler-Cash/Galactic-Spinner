Galactic Spinner

Galactic spinner is a game that was written in the graphically focused framework Processing. In this game the player controls a spaceship, this spaceship can only fly in a circular motion. It is up to the player to navigate the spaceship into the wormhole to increase their score. If a player hits a black hole the game will end, their score will be logged to the scoreboard.
